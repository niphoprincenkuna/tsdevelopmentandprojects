About Us

About TS Developments and Projects

Learn more about our story, our values, and the people behind your trusted business partner

Building Trust, Delivering Results

TS Developments and Projects was founded with a clear vision: to simplify the complex world of business registrations and compliance for entrepreneurs and organizations across South Africa.

Over the years, we have grown from a small consultancy into a trusted partner for hundreds of businesses. Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and client satisfaction has been the driving force behind everything we do.

Whether you are launching a new startup, registering a non-profit organization, or ensuring your business stays compliant with SARS and CIPC regulations, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

To empower South African businesses by providing reliable, efficient, and affordable registration and compliance services that allow our clients to focus on growth and success.
